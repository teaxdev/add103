    Satoru's Ninjas

    Everyoneâ€™s a Ninja now: How a silly ninja guy makes everyone a Ninja! Satoru Umezawa is an interesting commander that gives all of your creature cards in your hand Ninjutsu. What is ninjutsu you may ask? An epic ninja ability that lets you substitute an attacking creature for one in your hand, which basically means trickery. You can basically switch a little guy for a massive guy that will deal a lot of damage! His colors are Dimir, which is black and blue.

    Bident of Thassa: Lets you draw a card whenever you deal damage to an opponent, and it can also force them to attack, which leaves them open for your honorary ninjas to go in and smack people.

    Sheoldred, Whispering One: Another powerful creature that allows you to bring back creatures from your graveyard, and forces opponents to sacrifice their creatures.

    Nezahal, Primal Tide: A big creature that lets you leech off your opponents and draw lots of cards. He lets you keep all of your cards as long as he's in play, and he can protect himself if you're willing to sacrifice some of your cards.

    Jin-Gitaxias, Core Augur: Another big dude that will get you tons of cards, and makes your opponents lose their own cards.

    Yuriko, The Tiger's Shadow: Not so much a big creature, but a scary threat that lets you draw a card and deal damage equal to its mana value. With the high mana values of our other honorary Ninjas, she can deal lots of damage to opponents.

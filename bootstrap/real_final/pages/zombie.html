    Dimir Zombies!

    Zombo Army: How my zombies do their zombo things!

    Wilhelt, The Rotcleaver is the commander of this deck. He makes it so that every zombie that dies under my control turns into a decayed zombie (basically a worse zombie). This deck is a Zombie tribal deck, which means that almost all of the cards in this deck are zombies, or relate to and buff zombies. The colors of this deck are Dimir, which means Black and Blue, which black focuses on death and bringing back things from the dead, and blue focuses on control and drawing cards.

    Death Baron makes all my creatures bigger and gives them deathtouch (any amount of damage they deal is enough to kill other creatures).

    Gray Merchant of Asphodel, more commonly known as Gary, he is able to whittle down opponents quickly by dealing damage equal to how many black pips (color pips are on the top right of a card) you have on the battlefield.

    Diregraf Colossus grows bigger every time you make a zombie and also gives you an extra zombie for your troubles.

    Rooftop Storm is a powerful card that lets me cast my zombies for free. Free zombies are great in this economy!

    Army of the damned: When you cast it, you get 13 zombies! While they won't be available to you immediately, if they can survive until your next turn, you'll be able to attack for a lot of damage.

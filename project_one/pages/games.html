Gaming has always been a big part of my life. Whether I’m throwing together a wild deck in Magic: The Gathering or jumping into co-op chaos with friends, I enjoy the experience. I’d call myself a casual gamer. In Magic, I like testing out different decks, and my favorite card is Sneaky Snacker, as it has my favorite card art.

Pokémon is another favorite, whether it’s collecting the cards or battling in the games. My favorite Pokémon game is Black 2. When it comes to video games, I play a little bit of everything. I appreciate the eerie, teamwork-focused atmosphere in Lethal Company and R.E.P.O, but I also enjoy the deep strategy and alt-history fun of Hearts of Iron 4.

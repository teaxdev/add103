Hello, I’m Alex, currently working on my associate’s degree in Software Application Design and Development. This is my first semester at MCC, and I have enjoyed my time here so far. Music is a big part of my life. I’m always alternating between different bands and artists. Right now, it’s the indie sound of Men I Trust, Clairo’s mellow tunes, The Marías’ dreamy sound, or Kendrick Lamar’s incredible storytelling. I also enjoy playing tabletop games like Magic: The Gathering and Pokémon. I’m also a fan of board games. As for video games, I get a kick out of the teamwork in Lethal Company and R.E.P.O, but I also love the big-brain strategy of Hearts of Iron 4.

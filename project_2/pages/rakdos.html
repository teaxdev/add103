Prosper, Tome Bound

Prosper, Tome Bound is the commander of this deck. He lets me exile a card until the end of my next turn, which basically means I have a one turn timer to cast that card or I lose it forever. His second ability gives you a treasure every time you cast a card from exile, which works off of his first ability! The focus of this deck is to cast cards from exile, to gain treasure tokens. Treasures can be sacrificed for a mana of any color, which is a powerful one-off effect that essentially gives you more resources to work with. His colors are Rakdos, which is black and red.

Prosper's Treasures: How a silly Dungeons and Dragons character makes lots of treasure!

Jeska's Will: This cheap spell can quickly put you in the lead in a game by providing you with tons of mana, and is considered one of the strongest cards in Commander.

Reckless Endeavor: This is a tricky spell that can give you tons of treasure (which you really want) and kill every other creature on the battlefield at the same time!

Marionette Master: One of the most powerful cards in the deck, it can deal lots of damage when you use your treasures for mana

Apex of Power: Similar to Jeska's Will, this card can get you a lot of spells into exile, and give you tons of mana to work with, which lets you make even more treasure!

Professional Face-Breaker: This card lets all of your creatures create treasures when they deal damage to opponents, and also lets you use your treasures to get more cards into exile (to make even more treasures!)

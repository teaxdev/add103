Welcome to my Magic: The Gathering site where I will discuss a little about my Magic collection and the favorite decks I own. Here is a little explanation of what MtG (Magic: The Gathering) is like. My favorite format is Commander, which is a 99 card singleton format, which basically means you can only have one of each card, as opposed to a maximum of 4.

And there is one card you choose as your commander! It must be a legendary creature, and it will be the only card in your deck that you're going to use every single game. It's a 4 player format, and it's probably the most casual friendly format, and the most popular format in MtG these days.

My favorite game store is GriffoNest games at the woodstock square. They have plenty of Star Wars, DnD, Pokemon and of course Magic: The Gathering products, and tons of events to play each!

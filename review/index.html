Oncle Jazz Album

Oncle Jazz is the 3rd album by the indie band Men I Trust. I have recently discovered them this month and I have been actively listening to their music since then.

Oncle Jazz contains my favorite song by Men I Trust, called "Numb".

The song gives out a unique vibe that stands out to me, past the bands already unique style.

It also has their most popular song
    "Show me how"
and another one of my favorites, "I hope to be around".
